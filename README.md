# 📊 HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition patterns to uncover key factors influencing turnover.  
An interactive dashboard was built using Power BI to help visualize trends and support data-driven HR decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Understand why employees are leaving the company  
- Identify high-risk groups based on age, salary, and experience  
- Provide actionable insights to improve employee retention  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI  
- Excel (Data Cleaning)  
- Data Visualization  
- DAX (for calculated metrics)

## 📊 Key Insights
- Employees aged **26–35** show the highest attrition  
- Peak attrition occurs at **~1 year of tenure (59 employees)**  
- Majority of exits fall under the **≤5K salary bracket**  

---

## 💡 Analysis & Interpretation
The analysis indicates that attrition is highest among **early-tenure employees**, suggesting that many employees leave after gaining initial experience.  
This trend may be driven by:
- Low starting salaries  
- Better external opportunities  
- Limited early-career growth  

---

## 📉 Business Impact
- Increased hiring and training costs  
- Loss of early-stage talent  
- Reduced workforce stability  

---

## 📈 Recommendations
- Improve compensation structure for entry-level roles  
- Introduce clear career progression paths  
- Strengthen onboarding and engagement strategies  
- Conduct regular employee feedback analysis
